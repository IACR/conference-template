Student Stipends

The Cryptography Research Fund allows us to waive the registration fee for all student presenters of an accepted paper. A limited number of stipends will be available to those students unable to obtain funding to attend the conference, and to students having an accepted paper that they will present. Students in under-represented groups are especially encouraged to apply. To apply, please ask your PhD supervisor or Department Head to email a brief justification to the General Chair.

Information on deadlines for the student stipend application is not currently available. Check back soon.
